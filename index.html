Our Story

We are farmers and Wholesalers

Farmed across our two Tasmanian farms and sourced locally, our produce at We Farm Tasmania® is proudly delivered fresh across Australia and internationally and has been since 2020.

Farmed

Farmed in Tasmania, we grow our produce in a mixture of climate controlled and outdoor environments.

Packed

Our product is packaged on site on demand by our staff and delivered directly from our farms and wholesalers.

Delivered

We ship our produce all around Australia servicing all states and territories and internationally.

Locally grown and sourced

Farmed in Nierinna Tasmania across our two farms, our produce is grown in one of the purest locations in the world. Our wholesalers are local too, which means you receive the freshest produce.

Services we provide

From farm to market

Did you know We Farm Tasmania® is one of the few farms that operates their farming and holds a place at Melbourne Market? We pride ourselves on this and that our produce is consistently fresh and our supply chain dependable.

Quality Control

Because we are always committed to ensuring our customers receive the best possible product from our farms and wholesalers, we consistently check quality and production at each stage in our supply chain.

Our Brands

Sourced from Japan and developed over 7 years through millions of dollars of R&D in Australia, Oishii launched in 2020, producing some of the finest, sweetest strawberries available today, dedicated to supplying only premium grade produce.

Established in 2020, Nierinna Mushrooms is currently the largest mushroom supplier in Tasmania and remains the only farm to produce exotic and agaricus bisporus mushrooms through its fully automated growing rooms commercially, at scale in the region.

Nierinna Honey produces premium quality raw honey right here in Tasmania. Founded in 2016, Nierinna has since grown and now utilises the services of beekeepers to collect the premium unheated honey we believe is one of the finest in quality and nutrition from Tasmania.

Herboria superfoods produces nutrient dense foods in a convenient form. Focusing on powdered mushroom and herb extractions and developed overseas in our GMP factory, our supplements are convenient, nutritious and easy to incorporate in your daily life.

Established in 1990, Nierinna Blueberry has grown to become one of the most well-known blueberry farms in Tasmania. Using modern day holland technology, Nierinna farms traditional and hydroponically grown fruit powered by a PRIVA system producing fruit that is both higher in quality and yield.

Nierinna Sprouts (rebranding as Pegasus Sprouts) specialises in growing and supplying sprouts, micro-greens and herbs all year round. We are proudly the largest microgreen and sprouts growers in Tasmania.
